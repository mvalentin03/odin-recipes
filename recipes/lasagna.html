Description

Lasagna is a beloved Italian dish consisting of layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, typically stacked with a rich, flavorful sauce made from ground meat (such as beef or pork) or vegetables, combined with tomatoes, onions, garlic, and aromatic herbs.
